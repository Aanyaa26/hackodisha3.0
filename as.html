Who we are

The web-based healthcare appointment system for residents in Odisha solves several critical problems related to healthcare accessibility and efficiency: Reduced Wait Times: Long waiting times are a common issue in healthcare facilities. This platform allows residents to schedule appointments online, reducing the need for them to physically wait in line at healthcare facilities. This, in turn, decreases the overall waiting times for patients.

Digital Divide: Odisha, like many regions, may have disparities in internet access and digital literacy. Not all residents may have access to the internet or the knowledge to use the online appointment system. Bridging this digital divide is a significant challenge.
